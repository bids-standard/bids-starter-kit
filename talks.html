Important

If you have prepared or presented anything about BIDS that you would like to share, let us know and we will add it to the the project or the relevant playlist on youtube!

Also if you know of other talks or slides that probably should be added, don’t hesitate.

To upload slideshows, please use slideshare for direct online viewing or Dropbox / Google Drive for a download link
